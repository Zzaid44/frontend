Top Destinations

Pyramids - Egypt
The only surviving ancient wonder. It is a huge pyramid built as a tomb for the Pharaoh Khufu in Egypt

Petra – Jordan
An ancient city carved into rose-colored rock. Famous for its beautiful façade called The Treasury.

Colosseum – Italy
A huge Roman amphitheater in Rome where ancient gladiator fights and public events were held.

Plitvice Lakes – Croatia
A group of turquoise lakes connected by beautiful waterfalls, surrounded by green forests.

Banff National Park – Canada
A stunning park with blue lakes, tall mountains, and wildlife.

Ha Long Bay – Vietnam
A bay filled with tall limestone islands covered with green trees.

Istanbul - Turkey
It is famous for its rich history, beautiful landscapes, delicious food, and vibrant culture. From ancient ruins to stunning beaches and busy cities, Turkey offers many amazing places to explore.
